How It Works

01

Assess Your Baseline

Before we build anything, we measure everything. Body composition scan. Resting metabolic rate. Schedule Bloodwork panel. Fitness screening.

You'll know exactly how much lean mass you're carrying, where your metabolism sits, and what your body needs before we write a single line of programming.

Already on a GLP-1? We start here specifically because we need your current body composition picture before another week on medication passes.

02

Build Your System

Based on your results, we design your strength program, nutrition targets and, for Package 1 clients, initiate your clinical evaluation through Asher Med. Their licensed physicians independently assess eligibility for GLP-1/GIP therapy and other peptide-based support, including protocols for recovery, metabolic health, performance, and body-composition goals when clinically appropriate.

Already working with an endocrinologist, cardiologist, or PCP? Bring the records. Asher Med can coordinate alongside your existing providers, so you do not have to switch care to get clinical oversight integrated with your training.

03

Execute Your Plan

Weekly 50 minute personal training sessions at private studio in near West End. Program updates every 4 weeks based on how your body is actually responding. Direct coach access throughout.

At 12 weeks, we rescan and retest so you see exactly what changed in fat mass, lean mass, and metabolic markers. Not just the scale.

Most clients continue past the initial 12 weeks. At the rescan, we review the data and plan the next phase. Transformation doesn't have an end date.
