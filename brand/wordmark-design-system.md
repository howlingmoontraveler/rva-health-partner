# RVA Health Partner — Wordmark Design System
> Complete brief for Claude (or any AI design tool) to generate the RVA Health Partner wordmark.
> Feed this document as the full design context before issuing any generation prompt.

---

## 1. BRAND IDENTITY OVERVIEW

**Brand name:** RVA Health Partner  
**Abbreviation / monogram:** RVAHP (use sparingly) or standalone "RVA Health Partner"  
**Brand category:** Premium health coaching — strength training + peptide therapy + GLP-1 support  
**Location:** Richmond, Virginia  
**Positioning:** Evidence-based, medically adjacent, results-driven. Not a gym. Not a clinic. A complete solution.  
**Brand archetype:** The Sage + The Ruler — authoritative knowledge combined with premium control and precision  
**Emotional register:** Confident, restrained, clinical warmth. Signals expertise without arrogance.

---

## 2. WORDMARK STYLE DIRECTION

### Primary Direction: Prestige Serif Wordmark
The wordmark should feel like a luxury medical institution, not a fitness brand. Think: Mayo Clinic meets Rolex. Clean, authoritative, timeless.

**Style:** Uppercase or mixed-case serif wordmark with a precise, minimal supporting mark or monogram  
**Weight:** Medium to semi-bold — never thin/hairline, never heavy slab  
**Geometry:** Slight optical refinements expected — not mechanical or digital-feeling  
**Personality:** Measured authority. Nothing playful, rounded, or energetic.

### What This Wordmark Is NOT
- Not a gym logo (no barbells, dumbbells, lightning bolts, or flex silhouettes)
- Not a medical cross or pharmacy red-cross
- Not a tech startup wordmark (no geometric sans, no gradient, no rounded corners)
- Not a spa logo (no lotus flowers, leaves, or flowing script)
- Not a weight loss brand (no before/after arrows, scales, or transformation imagery)

## 4. TYPOGRAPHY

### Recommended Typefaces (in order of preference)

**Option A — Playfair Display**
- Style: High-contrast editorial serif, strong thick-thin stroke variation
- Weight: Medium (500) or SemiBold (600)
- Case: ALL CAPS for "RVA", Title Case for "Health Partner"
- Why: Established authority, luxury publishing feel, high legibility at all sizes
- Google Fonts: Free, widely supported

**Option B — Cormorant Garamond**
- Style: Refined transitional serif, elegant proportions
- Weight: SemiBold (600) or Bold (700)
- Case: Small caps or mixed case
- Why: Old-world prestige, refined without being cold
- Google Fonts: Free

**Option C — EB Garamond**
- Style: Classic humanist serif, slightly warmer than Cormorant
- Weight: Medium (500)
- Case: Mixed case, title case
- Why: Intellectual authority, editorial trust signal
- Google Fonts: Free

**Option D — Freight Display Pro** *(if licensed)*
- Style: Contemporary serif with classical roots
- Weight: Book or Medium
- Why: Used by premium health and finance brands

### Typeface Rules
- Never use sans-serif for the primary wordmark (sans-serif is for UI/body only)
- Never use script or display fonts
- Never use condensed or extended variants
- Lettertracking: +10 to +30 for "RVA" when used in caps; 0 to +5 for "Health Partner"
- Baseline: optically aligned, not mechanically centered

## 5. WORDMARK LOCKUP OPTIONS

The brand requires four lockup configurations. All four must be designed.

### Lockup 1 — Horizontal Full Wordmark (Primary)
```
[OPTIONAL MARK]  RVA HEALTH PARTNER
```
- "RVA" in forest green, slightly larger or bolder
- "HEALTH PARTNER" in antique gold OR charcoal at a slightly smaller scale (80–90% of RVA size)
- A thin horizontal rule (`#A37E2C`, 0.5–1px) may separate RVA from HEALTH PARTNER if they share the same line
- Optional: small location tag "RICHMOND, VA" in all-caps, tracked, at 50% size below the full wordmark

### Lockup 2 — Stacked Wordmark
```
      RVA
HEALTH PARTNER
```
- "RVA" centered, large, forest green
- "HEALTH PARTNER" centered below, antique gold, smaller tracked caps
- Thin gold rule between the two lines (optional)
- Location tag "RICHMOND, VA" optionally below

### Lockup 3 — Monogram / Icon Mark
```
[RHP]  or  [R]
```
- Lettermark using "R", "RH", or "RHP"
- Set in the primary serif, contained in a minimal geometric shape (circle, square, or shield — see Section 6)
- Forest green fill with antique gold letterform, OR forest green letterform on ivory
- This is the favicon, app icon, and social profile avatar

### Lockup 4 — Compact Horizontal (for nav/header)
```
RVA HEALTH PARTNER
```
- Single line, no mark, slightly tracked
- For website nav bar use — must work at 160px–240px wide

## 6. ICON / MARK OPTIONS

If a supporting graphic mark is used alongside the wordmark, it must conform to these options only:

### Option A — Geometric Shield
- A minimal shield outline (3–5 straight sides, no curves except one top arc)
- Represents protection, strength, and accountability
- Interior: monogram "R" or "RHP" in serif
- Stroke weight: 1–1.5px at standard size; never filled solid
- Colors: forest green outline, gold monogram on white/ivory

### Option B — Precision Cross / Plus
- A clean, equal-arm plus/cross mark — medical reference, precision
- Arms should be slightly elongated (not perfectly square)
- NOT the red medical cross — forest green only
- Paired with the wordmark only; not used alone

### Option C — Minimal Crown / Coronet
- A very restrained, geometric crown — 3 points, sharp, no curves
- References Rolex crown heritage without copying it
- Forest green, gold, or reversed ivory
- Must be redrawn from scratch — no crown clip art

### Option D — No Mark (Text-Only)
- The wordmark alone, no supporting mark
- Preferred for web and document use
- Cleanest, most confident option — recommended default

---

## 7. SPACING & CLEAR SPACE RULES

- Minimum clear space around the wordmark: equal to the cap height of the "R" in "RVA" on all sides
- Never crowd the wordmark with other text or imagery
- Minimum digital size: 120px wide for horizontal lockup; 40px wide for monogram
- Minimum print size: 1.25 inches wide for horizontal lockup

---

## 8. FORBIDDEN TREATMENTS

Do NOT apply any of the following to the wordmark under any circumstances:

- Drop shadows or outer glows
- Gradients on the wordmark itself (flat color only)
- Outlines or strokes added around letterforms
- Distortion, skewing, or stretching
- Rasterization at small sizes (always use SVG/vector)
- Placement on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid color block behind the mark
- Color combinations outside the approved palette
- Decorative swashes, underlines, or ornaments not specified in this document
- Lowercase-only treatment
- Emoji, icons, or decorative punctuation within or adjacent to the wordmark

## 10. AI GENERATION PROMPT (for Claude, Midjourney, or similar)

Use this prompt verbatim or as a base when generating the wordmark:

```
Design a premium wordmark for "RVA Health Partner," a luxury health coaching brand in Richmond, Virginia offering strength training, peptide therapy, and GLP-1 wellness support.

TYPOGRAPHY: Use a high-contrast editorial serif typeface (Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond style). "RVA" should appear slightly larger or bolder than "Health Partner."

LAYOUT: Horizontal lockup with "RVA" in forest green (#006039) and "HEALTH PARTNER" in antique gold (#A37E2C), or both in forest green with a thin gold rule separator. Clean, generous letter spacing. Optional small-caps "RICHMOND, VA" tagline below in tracked caps at 50% size.

ICON (optional): A minimal geometric shield or precision crown monogram containing the letter "R" — forest green outline, gold letterform, on white background. Geometric, no curves except necessary, never decorative.

STYLE: Rolex meets Mayo Clinic. Authoritative, restrained, medically adjacent. No gym imagery, no gradients, no drop shadows, no script fonts, no wellness clichés (no leaves, lotus, or cross).

COLORS: Forest Green #006039, Antique Gold #A37E2C, Deep Charcoal #1A1A1A, Ivory #F5F0E8, White #FFFFFF. Flat color only.

DELIVERABLE: Vector-style SVG or high-resolution PNG on transparent background. Also provide reversed version (ivory wordmark on forest green background).
```
